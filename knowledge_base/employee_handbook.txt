3. WORK SCHEDULE & ATTENDANCE
===========================================

Standard Working Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (8 hours + 1 hour lunch)
- Total: 40 hours per week
- Lunch Break: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

Flexible Working Hours:
Employees may choose flexible start times between 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM, as long as they complete 8 working hours daily. Please coordinate with your team lead.

Attendance Policy:
- Employees are expected to be punctual and maintain good attendance
- Tardiness: Arriving more than 15 minutes late without notice
- More than 3 tardiness incidents per month may result in performance review
- Absences must be reported to your manager before 9:00 AM

Remote Work Days:
- Eligible after 3 months of employment
- Up to 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday preferred)
- Must be approved by team lead
- Full details in Section 7: Remote Work Policy

4. LEAVE POLICIES
===========================================

Annual Leave:
- 15 working days per year
- Accrued monthly (1.25 days per month)
- Can be taken after 3 months of employment
- Maximum carry-over: 5 days to next year
- Must be submitted 2 weeks in advance for approval

Sick Leave:
- 12 working days per year
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Can be used for personal illness or caring for immediate family members
- Unused sick leave does not carry over

Public Holidays:
- All Indonesian national and religious holidays
- Approximately 16-18 days per year
- Announced annually by HR department

Maternity Leave:
- 3 months (90 days) fully paid
- Additional 1 month unpaid leave available upon request
- Must notify HR at least 2 months before expected due date

Paternity Leave:
- 10 working days fully paid
- Must be taken within 1 month of child's birth
- Requires birth certificate submission

Marriage Leave:
- 3 working days for employee's own marriage
- 2 working days for immediate family member's marriage (children, siblings, parents)
- Must be submitted at least 1 week in advance

Bereavement Leave:
- 3 working days for immediate family (parents, spouse, children, siblings)
- 1 working day for extended family (grandparents, in-laws)
- No advance notice required, but must inform manager as soon as possible

Unpaid Leave:
- Available after exhausting all paid leave
- Maximum 5 days per year
- Requires written approval from HR Director
- Not eligible during probation period

5. BENEFITS & COMPENSATION
===========================================

Salary:
- Paid monthly on the 25th of each month
- Bank transfer to employee's designated account
- Salary slips available on employee portal
- Annual salary review in January

Health Insurance:
- Comprehensive medical coverage for employee and immediate family
- Partner hospitals: 50+ hospitals nationwide
- Coverage: Outpatient, inpatient, dental, optical
- Family definition: Spouse + up to 3 children
- Premium fully covered by company

BPJS (Government Social Security):
- BPJS Kesehatan (Health): Company contribution 4%, Employee 1%
- BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (Employment): Company contribution as per regulation
- Automatic enrollment upon joining

Meal Allowance:
- IDR 50,000 per working day
- Paid monthly with salary
- Not applicable for remote work days

Transportation Allowance:
- IDR 30,000 per working day for on-site work
- OR company shuttle service from designated points
- Shuttle routes: Kelapa Gading, BSD, Bekasi, Bogor

Annual Bonus:
- Based on company performance and individual KPIs
- Typically 1-2 months salary
- Paid in January for previous year's performance
- Prorated for employees joining mid-year

Professional Development:
- Annual training budget: IDR 10,000,000 per employee
- Can be used for courses, certifications, conferences
- Must be related to job function
- Requires manager approval

Laptop & Equipment:
- Company-provided laptop (MacBook or ThinkPad)
- Monitor, keyboard, mouse for office setup
- Mobile phone allowance: IDR 500,000/month for senior roles
- Equipment must be returned upon resignation

Work from Home Setup:
- One-time allowance: IDR 3,000,000
- For ergonomic chair, desk, or other home office equipment
- Eligible after passing probation

Gym Membership:
- Company partners with fitness centers
- 50% subsidy up to IDR 300,000/month
- List of partner gyms available on employee portal

Team Building:
- Quarterly team outings and activities
- Annual company retreat
- Department budgets for team bonding activities

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
===========================================

Professional Behavior:
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain professional communication in all interactions
- Dress code: Smart casual (jeans acceptable, no shorts/sandals)
- Friday casual: More relaxed attire acceptable

Confidentiality:
- All company information is confidential
- Client data must be protected per NDA agreements
- No sharing of proprietary code, designs, or business strategies
- Violation may result in immediate termination and legal action

Conflict of Interest:
- Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest
- No accepting gifts or favors from clients/vendors exceeding IDR 500,000
- No working for competitors or starting competing businesses
- Side projects must be approved by management

Harassment Policy:
- Zero tolerance for harassment of any kind
- Sexual, racial, or discriminatory behavior is strictly prohibited
- Report incidents to HR immediately
- Confidential investigation process
- Whistleblower protection guaranteed

Drug & Alcohol Policy:
- Zero tolerance for drugs in workplace
- Alcohol only permitted at company-sanctioned events
- Employees under influence will be sent home
- Repeated violations lead to termination

Data Security:
- Use strong passwords (changed every 90 days)
- No sharing of login credentials
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Report security incidents immediately
- No use of personal USB drives with company computers

Social Media Guidelines:
- Personal social media use is permitted
- Do not share confidential company information
- Do not speak on behalf of company without authorization
- Maintain professional image when identifying as TechVision employee

7. REMOTE WORK POLICY
===========================================

Eligibility:
- Completed probation period (3 months)
- Good performance record
- Role suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required

Remote Work Days:
- Maximum 2 days per week
- Preferred days: Tuesday and Thursday
- Must be scheduled in advance (at least 1 day notice)
- Team leads may set team-specific policies

Requirements:
- Stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Quiet, professional work environment
- Available on Slack/Teams during working hours
- Attend all scheduled meetings via video call

Equipment:
- Company laptop must be used
- VPN connection required for accessing systems
- Keep all software updated
- IT support available remotely

Expectations:
- Same productivity standards as office work
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes during work hours
- Video must be on during meetings
- Submit daily progress updates to manager

Remote Work Allowance:
- IDR 200,000 per month for internet and electricity
- Paid with monthly salary
- Only for approved remote work days

Termination of Remote Work Privilege:
- Consistent missed deadlines
- Poor communication or unavailability
- Failure to maintain productivity
- May be revoked at manager's discretion

Full Remote Work:
- Available only for specific roles (approved by HR Director)
- Must live within 4 hours of Jakarta for occasional office visits
- Quarterly in-person meetings required
- Separate policy document available
